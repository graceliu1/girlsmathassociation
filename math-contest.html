2020 GMA Math Contest

WELCOME
Welcome to the first annual 2020 GMA Math Contest! This online competition is designed to be an opportunity for young girls to heighten their interest in STEM fields. It is important to provide an alternative to the negative perceptions girls might develop at a young age and lead them to embrace math and science when they reach high school.

​The Girls Math Association believes participating in competitions at earlier ages will give girls a chance to step out of gender stereotypes and gain more confidence in their STEM skills. ​

Read more information about the contest and registration below.

EVENT OVERVIEW
DATE: The 2020 GMA Math Contest will be held from August 1-3.

LOCATION: Due to the current COVID-19 situation, the contest will be held online. Registered contestants will receive the problems and a submission form at the beginning of the contest period. Contestants from any country are welcome to apply, as long as they have access to Google and can read English. 

EXAM FORMAT: The exam will consist of 30 multiple choice problems designed to be solved in 50 minutes. The difficulty of the exam will be relatively easier than the American Mathematics Contest 8 (AMC 8). ***For more information about the exam, scroll down to the "Exam Details and More" section!***

OTHER RULES: Calculators are NOT allowed. The only aids permitted are pens, pencils, graph paper, regular paper, compass, and rulers. ​

REGISTRATION: Scroll down below for the requirements! Please complete this form.

​PRIZES: We are considering scores in two separate categories: Grades 5-6 and Grades 7-8. The top three scorers in each category will receive monetary awards.
REGISTRATION
  • There is no registration fee. 
  • ​People who identify as female are welcome to participate in the competition. 
  • Contestants must be in grades 5-8 for the 2020-2021 school year. 
  • Please complete this form. We will require a parental signature.

EXAM DETAILS AND MORE!
Not all of our contestants have previous experience with math competitions, and we respect that! This section is meant to provide a brief overview of the details of the test and what to expect.

The difficulty of the test will be similar or slightly easier than the American Mathematics Contest 8 (AMC 8). The AMC 8 is a popular competition for many students in middle school. The general topics covered include algebra, geometry, number theory, and counting and probability. The problems on our contest will cover these topics in limited scope.

A little intimidated? Don't worry, you don't need to master all of the topics to receive recognition in our contest! The purpose of our contest is to encourage girls to gain exposure to mathematics and STEM from an earlier age, so we have also divided participants into two categories. Students in grades 5-6 will have separate consideration from students in grades 7-8. 

Still have questions about how to get more involved with math as an elementary or middle school student? We're adding an informative post to our blog with specific details on math progression in the Dublin Unified School District. Check it out for more information!
